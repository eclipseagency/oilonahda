Preamble

A monthly partnership

This Agreement governs the relationship between Eclipse Agency ("First Party") and Oilo Spa ("Second Party") for the management of the brand's social media presence and the production of creative content, under the terms set out below.

1 · The Parties

First Party: Eclipse Agency — a content and digital-performance studio.

Second Party: Oilo Spa — a men's spa located in Riyadh, KSA.

2 · Scope of Engagement

The First Party shall manage Oilo Spa's accounts on Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, Facebook, and X (Twitter), and produce a complete monthly content programme (photography direction, design, video, copy) aligned with the brand's identity, with daily engagement and a monthly performance report.

"The aim is a refined digital presence that translates into real visits to the spa — not vanity numbers."

3 · Term

This Agreement begins on the date of signature and renews monthly by default, unless either party gives the other fifteen (15) days' written notice of non-renewal before the end of the current month.

Scope of Services

What the First Party delivers

The monthly retainer covers the following — all included at no additional charge:

Content & Creative

  • Monthly content plan aligned to brand and goals
  • 18 designed posts across the active channels
  • 15 stories distributed weekly / daily
  • 2 short-form videos / reels with edit & sound
  • Caption writing in concise classical Arabic
  • Hashtag strategy tailored for Riyadh audience
  • Photography direction + monthly shot list
  • Highlight covers & profile graphics on demand

Management & Engagement

  • Scheduling & publishing through Publer
  • DM responses within business hours
  • Comment moderation & daily engagement
  • Review monitoring & customer-review nudges
  • Promo coordination with the spa management
  • Seasonal coverage (Eid, National Day, etc.)
  • Customer-service support via Oilo WhatsApp
  • Monthly performance report in editorial format

Channels covered

Instagram · TikTok · Snapchat · Facebook · X (Twitter).
Excluded from scope: external on-site shoots, paid advertising spend (billed separately), and website / app design.
